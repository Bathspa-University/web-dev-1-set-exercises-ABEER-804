Plot

we've got Captain Joe Glass, an American submarine captain. He's out there in the Arctic Ocean, searching for a distressed U.S. submarine. But He stumbles upon a super-secret Russian coup plot! it's a conspiracy that could totally mess up the whole world order. Now, to save his crew and his country, Captain Glass has to gather an elite team of Navy SEALs. These guys are gonna have to be sneaky, manoeuvre through enemy waters, and pull off a daring mission to rescue the kidnapped Russian president. And the cherry on top? They gotta prevent World War III from happening! Talk about intense, It's a race against time, filled with suspense and high-stakes action.

Key Information

In this movie, Joe Glass, the captain of the American submarine, teams up with the US Navy Seals to rescue the Russian president who was taken hostage during a secret military coup. It sounds like an intense and action-packed storyline! The movie was released on October 19, 2018, in the UK, and it was directed by Donovan Marsh. It made around 31.7 million USD at the box office and was distributed by Lionsgate Films. The movie is based on the book "Firing Point" by Don Keith and George Wallace. The budget for the film was around $40 million.
