Key Information

In this movie, Joe Glass, the captain of the American submarine, teams up with the US Navy Seals to rescue the Russian president who was taken hostage during a secret military coup. It sounds like an intense and action-packed storyline! The movie was released on October 19, 2018, in the UK, and it was directed by Donovan Marsh. It made around 31.7 million USD at the box office and was distributed by Lionsgate Films. The movie is based on the book "Firing Point" by Don Keith and George Wallace. The budget for the film was around $40 million.
