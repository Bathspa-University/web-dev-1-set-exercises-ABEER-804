Chicken Biryani

Spicy Rice Meat Cooked in Aromatic Spices

Ingredients

  • Chicken Meat-500g
  • Basmati Rice(Soak for 20 Min)-500g
  • Onion(medium,thinly sliced)-3
  • Tomato(medium,thinly sliced)-3
  • Yogurt - 250g
  • Garlic paste - 2tbsp
  • Ginger paste - 2tbsp
  • Oil - 3/4 Cup

Cooking Metod

  1. Fry onions in hot oil until golden.Add meat,ginger and garlic.Stir fry for 5 minutes.Add Yogurt and Biryani Masala.Stir fry for 5 minutes on medium heat
  2. Add 1 cup of water for chicken.Cover and cook on low heat untill the meat is tender.
  3. Add tomatoes.Stir fry on high heat until the oil separates
  4. Separately,boil the soaked rice in 12 cup of water with 3 tablespoons of salt until 3/4 done.Then drain thoroughly.
  5. Spread some oil from the gravy on the bottom of a pot.Add a layer of half the rice,then pour the meat cury over it.Top with another layer of rice. Sprinkle yellow food colour it desired
  6. Cover and cook on high heat for 3 minutes.Reduce heat and cook for another 10 minutes.
  7. Serve with raita and salad.
